# {% include icon.html icon="fa-solid fa-users" %}Team

Our lab is built as a collaborative, supportive, and ambitious research community. We believe that diverse teams do better science, and we work to create an environment where expectations are transparent, mentorship is thoughtful, and researchers from different backgrounds feel welcomed, respected, and able to thrive. The team will include postdoctoral researchers, students at multiple stages, staff, and collaborators who share a commitment to rigorous, open, and inclusive science. Interested in joining the lab? Visit the [Join page](https://jhalab.org/join/) for information on applying.
